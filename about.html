Our Story

Our Story

Nepal Adventures was founded in 2009 by Pemba Sherpa, a native of the Everest region who grew up watching international climbers and trekkers pursue their dreams in the Himalayas. After working as a porter and guide for various companies, Pemba realized there was a need for more authentic, responsible, and personalized trekking experiences.

What started as a small family business has grown into one of Nepal's most respected adventure tourism companies. We've maintained our commitment to authentic experiences, environmental responsibility, and supporting local communities while ensuring the highest safety standards for our clients.

Today, we're proud to have guided over 2,000 adventurers from around the world, helping them discover not just the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas, but also the warmth and richness of Nepalese culture.
