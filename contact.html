Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about trekking in Nepal

What's the best time to trek in Nepal?

The best times are pre-monsoon (March-May) and post-monsoon (September-November) when skies are clear and weather is stable.

Do I need previous trekking experience?

We offer treks for all levels. Our guides will assess your fitness and recommend suitable routes. Basic fitness and preparation are recommended.

What permits do I need?

We handle all permits including TIMS cards and national park permits. You just need a valid passport and visa.

Is travel insurance required?

Yes, comprehensive travel insurance including helicopter evacuation coverage is mandatory for all our treks.
